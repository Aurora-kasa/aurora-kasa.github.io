Project-X | Game Studio Introduction
Project-X is a tight-knit game studio built by developers who grew up on pixels and stayed for the possibilities. We treat every project like a lab experiment: rapid prototypes, player feedback in 24 h, and the courage to kill our darlings if they don't sparkle. Our mission is simple—"Playable Wonder, Delivered Fast."
What We Do
Micro-Scope Games – 1-to-3-month prototypes that test wild mechanics; if it's fun, we scale it.
Mid-Core IPs – polished PC / console titles with a twist, built in Unity & Unreal, shipped in under two years.
Co-Creation Tools – we open-source our best in-house editor extensions and shaders back to the community.
Core Values
Player-First Loop – private Discord builds every Friday; patches drop Monday.
Tech Curiosity – we prototype on Godot, ship on Unreal, and script our own CI in Rust for fun.
Zero Crunch Policy – sustainable velocity beats hero weekends.
Team & Culture
12 full-time "X-ers" across three time zones; flat structure, shared revenue pool, mandatory play-hour daily at 3 p.m. UTC.
Project-X – we don't just make games; we press start on the unexpected.
